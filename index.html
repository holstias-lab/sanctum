📗 How to Use The Sanctum

Your complete guide to everything

🍅
Focus Timer — The Main Loop
Go to Focus Timer in the sidebar. Pick your language and session type, then hit ▶ Start. Study for 25 minutes, and when the timer ends — +25 XP is automatically added and your session is logged. No need to do anything. Take the 5-minute break when it switches, then repeat. Every 4 cycles you get a longer 15-minute break.
⚔️
Quests — Your Goals
Go to Quests. You'll see 5 active quests and some locked ones. When you complete a quest in real life (e.g. you can now read Al-Fatiha fluently), click the quest card to mark it complete and claim the XP. Use ✏️ Edit mode to rename, edit, add or delete quests to match your personal goals.
📖
Vocabulary — Adding Words
Go to Vocabulary. 15 Quranic Arabic starter words are already there. To add more, click the + Add Word button in the top right. Enter the word (in Arabic script or any language), its meaning, pronunciation, and an example sentence. Hit Save — +2 XP instantly. Use the filters to browse by language or mastery level.
🃏
Flashcards — Review Words
Go to Flashcards. Pick a language, hit Start. Tap the card to flip it and see the meaning. Hit ✓ Got it to level up that word's mastery (New → Learning → Reviewing → Mastered). When a word reaches Mastered, you get +5 XP. The more you review, the more XP you earn.
🕌
Quran Progress — Track Surahs
Go to Quran. You'll see 20 surahs (Al-Fatiha + Juz' Amma). Each surah has 3 checkboxes: Read (can read the Arabic), Memory (memorised it), Meaning (understand the translation). Tick each one as you achieve it — each tick earns XP automatically.
📜
Tajweed — Recitation Rules
Go to Tajweed. You'll find 7 core rules explained in plain English with Arabic examples. Once you've practised a rule and feel confident, click Mark as mastered for +10 XP. Use this as your reference while studying the Quran.
✏️
Editing Everything
Click the ✏️ Edit button in the top-right corner. While edit mode is on:
• Click any text to rename it
• Click your portrait or any cover image to change it (paste a URL from Unsplash)
• Click any quest to edit its name, description, XP, or delete it
• Add new quests with the + Add Quest button
• Edit your daily ritual items the same way
Everything saves automatically to your Supabase database.
💡
Tips for Getting Images
Go to unsplash.com — it's a free image site. Find an image you like, right-click it and choose "Copy image address". Then in Edit mode, click the portrait or cover and paste that URL. Dark moody images work best for the aesthetic — try searching "dark library", "old books", "candle", "mosque", "arabic calligraphy".
