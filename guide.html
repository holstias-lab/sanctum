Guide

How to make the most of your Nhoma.

dashboard

Language Hub

Your home base. Track the languages you're learning, review your Featured Word of the Day, and complete your Daily Ritual to build streaks and climb the Rank Ladder.

timer

Focus Timer

A 25-minute Pomodoro timer for deep study. Completing a focus phase earns +25 XP and logs a session automatically.

menu_book

Vocabulary & Flashcards

Add words to your Vocabulary Bank, then drill them in Flashcards. Rating a card as "Good" or "Easy" raises its mastery score.

auto_stories

Quran Reader

Read any of the 114 surahs with Arabic text and an English translation, pulled live from the Al Quran Cloud API. Finishing a surah for the first time earns +30 XP.

spellcheck

Tajweed

Reference the core rules of recitation and mark each one as practiced to earn XP.

bolt

XP & Ranks

XP accumulates from focus sessions, mastering words, quests, and Quran reading. Ranks unlock at 500, 1,500, and 3,500 XP. Everything is saved locally in your browser.
